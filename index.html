About Us

Jash spoken english & ielts training

Jash Spoken English & IELTS Training is a highly rated language institute located in the heart of Perambur, Chennai. With a strong focus on practical communication skills, we help students, professionals, and aspiring migrants master the English language and achieve their target IELTS scores. Our experienced trainers use proven methodologies to build confidence, fluency, and accuracy in every learner.

Whether you are a beginner looking to improve your everyday English or a candidate preparing for IELTS to study or work abroad, Jash Training Centre offers structured programs tailored to your goals. Conveniently located near the vegetable market and Ebenezer Stores in Kakkanji Colony, we welcome learners six days a week from early morning to evening, making it easy to fit quality learning into your busy schedule.

  • Highly rated institute with 4.7/5 stars — trusted by students across Chennai
  • Expert coaching for both Spoken English and IELTS band score improvement
  • Flexible batch timings six days a week from 7:30 AM to 8:30 PM
